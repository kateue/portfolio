MindJot

Role → Sole Product Designer

Timeline → Jan 2024–Mar 2024

MindJot -- an app to journal in a fluid and thoughtful way

Background context

The project originated with the goal of creating an AI-powered journaling technology aimed at fostering emotional and psychological self-reflection.

Therefore, I came up with MindJot, a specialized journaling app designed to cater to individual users. Leveraging machine learning, it offers a personalized interface to promote a more seamless and introspective journaling journey for all writers.

introduction

02

Research

User Interviews

Journaling has been extensively documented as an effective tool for managing anxiety, stress, emotional turmoil, difficulty concentrating, memory lapses, and creative blocks. To delve deeper into the practical applications of journaling, I conducted interviews with 6 individuals experienced in navigating these emotional hurdles. Rather than focusing solely on their journaling habits, my inquiries centered around the interplay of their emotions in their daily lives and how journaling could seamlessly integrate into their coping strategies.

Interview Insights

Surprisingly, all six of my interviewees expressed either a negative or neutral attitude towards journaling. It was perplexing to discover that something which has brought me immense positive benefits appeared as a chore to them. Guided vs Unguided Chats

In a collaborative research study conducted by the University of Washington and Northwestern University, titled “Suddenly, we got to become therapists for each other”, researchers investigated various forms of talk therapy aimed at improving mental health. Their findings revealed two distinct types of chat interventions that were effective in reducing anxiety: guided and unguided chats.

In the context of personal writing through journaling, I found inspiration in the concept of guided and unguided chatting from the research study.

Just like the black squiggly line in the center, which represents a balance between guided and unguided writing, this approach can offer both structured support and open exploration. It allows for the discovery of solutions and new perspectives while maintaining a lighthearted and emotionally smooth experience.

Keys to Effective Journaling

Professor James Pennebaker, an esteemed American social psychologist and staunch advocate for journaling, highlights a couple of crucial principles for effective journaling. These principles can be distilled into two key points: intentionality and conciseness.

 
 
 
 
 

03

Ideation

To enrich my design process and ideation, I developed several personas that embody various emotional challenges that journaling can address. Drawing from research and reflection on these personas, I proceeded to brainstorm design solutions tailored to alleviate the specific issues faced by each persona.

 
 
 
 

04

Userflow

Creating a low-fidelity user flow was instrumental in outlining the journey through MindJot, from onboarding to completion. My primary emphasis was on crafting a personalized and tailored journaling experience for every user. This user flow provided a foundational framework for ensuring that each step of the journey resonated with the individual needs and preferences of our users.

 
 
 
 

05

Stylescape

Drawing inspiration from various sources, I explored typography, color palettes, and compositional techniques to cultivate a tranquil and soothing ambiance for MindJot. My aim is to evoke a sense of coziness akin to curling up with a pen, pad, and blanket in front of a crackling fireplace after a restful day. I crafted a stylescape to encapsulate this atmosphere and ensure that writing with MindJot feels like a comforting retreat for users.

in MindJot...

The primary focus of this project was to develop a design system from the ground up. I encountered challenges in the workplace where designing within existing structured systems limited creative freedom. I realized that crafting components and guidelines from scratch differs significantly from simply using pre-existing assets.

By creating my own components and style guide, I gained insights into designing products systematically and with meticulous attention to detail. Establishing standards, from the spacing between elements to font variations and color palettes, reinforced industry practices and underscored the importance of consistency. This experience serves as a valuable reminder for future personal projects, as I aim to maintain this level of precision and thoughtfulness in my design approach.
